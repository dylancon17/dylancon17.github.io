About Me

Jennele is a self taught baker and cake designer located in Calgary, Alberta. While living in England, Jennele started to bake and design cakes for friends and family. Now back in Canada, her passion to push the caking limits drives her delicious creations.

Fun Facts!!!

1.) Born in St. John's Newfoundland, Jennele has travelled and lived all over the world.
2.) Teacher and musician by day, cake artist by night!
3.) Jennele and her husband Sam have 2 gorgeous, creative kids.
4.) Blush Bridal Magazine featured one of Jennele's elegant wedding cakes.
5.) She has a serious addiction to sour gummies.

Dedicated...

To ensure that everyone has the cake of their dreams for their special celebration.
